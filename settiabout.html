About Us

Director of SETTI

Self-Employment Technical Training Institute trains and fills the big vacuum of skilled and trained young and boys and girls. It also focuses the deprived and underprivileged adolescent with semiskilled and skilled tests and certification so as to bring them in the main stream of development of Bangladesh.

Since beginning, SETTI minutely follows up the training curricula and the employment opportunity of various course qualified professionals both at home and abroad. Employment and job placement of those qualifying from SETTI is one of our prime concerns.
